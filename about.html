About Our Department

Leading the future of computer science education and research

Overview

The Department of Computer Science is a vibrant academic community dedicated to advancing the field of computer science through excellence in education, research, and service. Established in 1985, we have grown to become one of the most respected computer science departments in the country.

Vision

To be a global leader in computer science education and research, fostering innovation and developing leaders who shape the digital future.

Mission

To provide exceptional education, conduct groundbreaking research, and serve society through technological innovation and leadership development.

Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, and Service to humanity through technology.

Our History

1985

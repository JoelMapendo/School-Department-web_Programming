How to Register

1

Login to Student Portal

Access the student portal using your university credentials.

2

Check Registration Status

Verify your eligibility and clear any holds on your account.

3

Select Courses

Choose courses from the available catalog based on your program requirements.

4

Review Schedule

Check for time conflicts and ensure you meet all prerequisites.

5

Submit Registration

Confirm your course selection and submit for approval.

6

Pay Fees

Complete tuition payment to finalize your registration.

Registration Guidelines

Payment Requirements

  • Minimum 50% tuition payment required for registration
  • Full payment due by add/drop deadline
  • Late registration fees apply after deadline
  • Payment plans available through finance office

Course Load Requirements

  • Undergraduate: 12-18 credit hours per semester
  • Graduate: 9-15 credit hours per semester
  • Overload requires advisor approval
  • Minimum 12 credits for full-time status

Important Notes

  • Registration is first-come, first-served
  • Popular courses fill up quickly
  • Prerequisites are strictly enforced
  • Waitlist available for closed courses

Advisor Approval

  • All registrations require advisor approval
  • Schedule meetings before registration period
  • Graduate students need committee approval
  • Thesis students need research advisor approval
